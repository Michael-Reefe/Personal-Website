Research Groups & Resources

Massachusetts Institute of Technology

The McDonald Group
  • A research group led by MIT professor Mike McDonald that explores galaxy clusters, galaxy formation, the intracluster medium, and AGN feedback.


    • George Mason University

    The Black Hole-Galaxy Connection (BGC) Group
  • A research group led by GMU professor Shobita Satyapal that investigates the interactions between AGNs and their host galaxies.

    • The Exoplaneteers
  • A research group led by GMU associate professor Peter Plavchan that focuses on exoplanet follow-up validation and characterization efforts.

    • GMU Observatory
  • The observatory that hosts the 0.8-meter telescope at GMU's main Fairfax campus.

    • Spectrum
  • A student-led organization whose goal is to promote diversity and inclusion for under-represented minorities in STEM.
